About Me

I am a third-year Computer Engineering Ph.D. student at Texas A&M University advised by Prof. Jeyavijayan Rajendran within the Secure and Trustworthy Hardware (SETH) Lab.
My research interests include the utilization and optimization of generative-AI tools, such as Large Language Models (LLMs), for hardware design and security applications.

My research is supported through the CSME/SCALE Traineeship program.

Prior to my graduate studies, I recieved my Bachelors in Computer Engineering with Minors in Mathematics and Cybersecurity from Texas A&M University in Spring 2023.

I am currently open to internship opportunities for the Summer 2026 period.
